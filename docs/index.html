Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Short version: Sound Tuner does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

Sound Tuner is developed and maintained as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses and how it is used.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This App is designed to function entirely on-device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • Audio recordings — microphone input is processed in real-time and immediately discarded

3. Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone solely to perform real-time pitch detection for the guitar tuner feature.

Audio captured from the microphone is processed locally on your device in real-time. It is never recorded, saved to disk, or transmitted anywhere. Once the tuner is closed, no audio data is retained.

4. Local Storage

The App saves your preferences (such as tuning settings, metronome BPM, and time signature) to your device's local storage using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting (such as Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, etc.).

The only platform interaction is with the Google Play Store for app distribution, which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy .

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the App is safe for use by all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or reach out via email at ramaaryasutaweihan@email.com.
